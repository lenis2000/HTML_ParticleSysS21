#### Proposition 4.0.4

The density $\rho(t,x)=\frac{1}{2}-\frac{x}{2t}$ solves the Burgers equation
$$
\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\rho(t,x)+\frac{\partial}{\partial x}
\left( \rho(t,x)\left( 1-\rho(t,x) \right) \right)=0.
$$
